Mixed Rice Dishes

Albaloo Polo

Basmati rice, sour cherries, chicken/meat/meat balls, saffron, and garnished with pistachio/almond slices.

Baghali Polo

A traditional Persian dish made with rice, fava beans, dill, and served with a side of lamb shank/chicken.

Loobia Polo

A hearty Persian dish served with a mixture of basmati rice, beef, green beans, onions, and tomato sauce.

Meygoo Polo

A unique dish from the coasts of the Persian gulf made with a combination of shrimp, rice, saffron, and other Persian spices.

Sabzi Polo

A healthy Persian dish with basmati rice, dill, parsley, saffron, and served with a side of fish (salmon/trout/tilapia).

Shirin Polo

A mixture of basmati rice, chicken, sweetened pistachios, almonds, Persian spices, and topped with carrot slices.

Zereshk Polo

An iconic Persian dish made with sauteed barberry, saffron, chicken, basmati rice, and garnished with pistachio slices.
